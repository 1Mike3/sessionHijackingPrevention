Cookie Policy

This site uses cookies to improve your experience and ensure the security of our testing mechanisms. Below is an overview of the types of cookies used:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be switched off.
  • Telemetry Cookies: Used for analytics to improve testing mechanisms. These are non-essential and require your explicit consent.

Consent and Your Choices

By clicking "Accept," you consent to the use of telemetry cookies. If you do not wish to consent, you may leave the site without clicking "Accept." Essential cookies will still function as required.

Data Collection

The site collects telemetry data, which may include:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser types
  • Operating systems
  • Session durations
  • And similar system data

This data is stored securely and used only for testing purposes. It will not be shared or published in any way. Data will be retained for a maximum of 12 months or until the testing project concludes, whichever is sooner.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data collected about you.
  • Request deletion or restriction of data usage.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.
